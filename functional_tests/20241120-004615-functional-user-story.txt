Functional Test Cases:

1. Credit Card Due Reminder:
   - Verify if an automated notification is sent to the cardholder reminding them of their upcoming credit card payment due date.
     Input: Due date is approaching. Expected output: Notification is sent.
   - Verify if the notification includes the last 4 digits of the credit card number for identification purposes.
     Input: Credit card number. Expected output: Last 4 digits of the credit card number in the notification.

2. Overdue Balance Alert:
   - Verify if an alert is sent to the cardholder if they miss their payment due date.
     Input: Payment due date is missed. Expected output: Alert is sent.
   - Verify if the alert includes the last 4 digits of the credit card number.
     Input: Credit card number. Expected output: Last 4 digits of the credit card number in the alert.

3. Collection Notification:
   - Verify if a formal collection notification is sent to the cardholder when an account becomes significantly delinquent.
     Input: Account becomes significantly delinquent. Expected output: Collection notification is sent.
   - Verify if the collection notification includes the last 4 digits of the credit card number.
     Input: Credit card number. Expected output: Last 4 digits of the credit card number in the notification.

4. Payment Plan Proposal:
   - Verify if a payment plan proposal is offered to the cardholder when they are unable to pay the full overdue balance at once.
     Input: Cardholder unable to pay full overdue balance. Expected output: Payment plan proposal is offered.
   - Verify if the payment plan proposal includes the last 4 digits of the credit card number.
     Input: Credit card number. Expected output: Last 4 digits of the credit card number in the proposal.

5. Collection Agency Involvement:
   - Verify if a collection agency is involved if the cardholder fails to respond to previous notifications and reminders.
     Input: Cardholder fails to respond. Expected output: Collection agency is involved.
   - Verify if the collection agency is provided with the last 4 digits of the credit card number for identification purposes.
     Input: Credit card number. Expected output: Last 4 digits of the credit card number provided to the collection agency.

6. Legal Action Initiation:
   - Verify if legal action is initiated against the cardholder in extreme cases of non-payment or default.
     Input: Extreme cases of non-payment or default. Expected output: Legal action initiated.
   - Verify if any legal documentation includes the last 4 digits of the credit card number for identification purposes.
     Input: Credit card number. Expected output: Last 4 digits of the credit card number in legal documentation.

Non-Functional Test Cases:

1. Security:
   - Verify if only the last 4 digits of the credit card number are included in any communication or documentation related to the collection process.
     Input: Credit card number. Expected output: Only the last 4 digits of the credit card number are included.
   - Verify if the full credit card number is never shared or displayed in plain text.
     Input: Credit card number. Expected output: Full credit card number is not shared or displayed.

2. Performance:
   - Verify if the system is able to handle the load when multiple notifications, alerts, and proposals are sent at the same time.
     Input: Multiple notifications, alerts, and proposals. Expected output: System is able to handle the load.

3. Usability:
   - Verify if the notifications, alerts, and proposals are easy to understand and actionable for the cardholder.
     Input: Notifications, alerts, and proposals. Expected output: Easy to understand and actionable.

4. Reliability:
   - Verify if the system is able to send notifications, alerts, and proposals reliably and on time.
     Input: Due dates, overdue balances, etc. Expected output: Notifications, alerts, and proposals sent reliably and on time.
